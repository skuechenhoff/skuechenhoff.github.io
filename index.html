Research

Neural representations of future states

DPhil project · 2022 – present

I study how neural populations encode abstract structure that supports flexible planning and schema generalisation. I use multivariate analyses and model-based approaches to link human fMRI and iEEG (LFP and spikes) to mechanistic theories of higher cognition.

Cross-species & cross-modal modelling

My work connects human data with complementary rodent recordings, enabling comparisons across species and scales. This allows me to test hypotheses about shared coding principles underlying planning and generalisation.

Previous projects

Max Planck · Oxford · LMU
